Getting Started with HomeLoop

HomeLoop is Kenya's premier real estate platform connecting home seekers with trusted agents and verified property listings. Whether you're looking to rent or buy, we make finding your dream home simple and secure.

What You Can Do on HomeLoop

  • Browse verified property listings across Kenya with detailed information and high-quality photos
  • Use advanced filters to find properties that match your specific needs (location, price, bedrooms, bathrooms)
  • Save your favorite properties for easy access later
  • Book property visits directly with agents at times that work for you
  • Chat with agents in real-time to ask questions about properties
  • Read and write reviews to help others make informed decisions
  • View agent profiles to learn about their experience and other listings

Searching for Properties

How to Search

  • Use the search bar at the top of any page to search by location, property type, or keywords
  • Click the search button or press Enter to view results
  • Browse through the listings with images, prices, and key details

Using Filters

  • Refine your search using filters for price range, number of bedrooms, bathrooms, and property type
  • Select your preferred location or neighborhood
  • Sort results by price (low to high or high to low), newest listings, or most popular

Viewing Property Details

  • Click on any property card to view full details including photos, description, and agent information
  • Scroll through the image gallery to see all available photos
  • Check the property specifications (bedrooms, bathrooms, size, amenities)
  • Read reviews from other users who have visited or inquired about the property

Using Favorites

Save properties you're interested in for easy access later.

Adding to Favorites

  • On any property details page, click the "Add to Favourites" button
  • You'll see a confirmation message when the property is saved
  • Access all your saved properties by clicking "Favourites" in the top navigation

Managing Your Favorites

  • View all your saved properties on the Favourites page
  • Remove properties you're no longer interested in by clicking the remove icon
  • Your favorites are saved locally in your browser (no account required, but they won't sync across devices)

Booking Property Visits

How to Book a Visit

  • On a property details page, click the "Book Visit" button
  • You'll need to be logged in to book a visit (create a free account if you don't have one)
  • Fill in the booking form with your preferred date and time
  • Add any special requests or questions in the notes section
  • Submit your booking request and wait for agent confirmation

After Booking

  • You'll receive a confirmation notification once the agent approves your visit
  • Check your email for booking details and reminders
  • The agent may contact you via the chat feature to confirm or adjust timing
  • If you need to reschedule or cancel, contact the agent directly through chat

During Your Visit

  • Arrive on time at the property address provided
  • The agent will meet you and show you around the property
  • Feel free to ask questions and take your time exploring
  • After your visit, you can leave a review to help other users

Chatting with Agents

Starting a Chat

  • On any property details page, click the "Chat Agent" button
  • A chat window will open on the right side of your screen
  • Type your message and click send or press Enter
  • The agent will respond as soon as they're available

What to Ask

  • Questions about property features, condition, or availability
  • Clarification on price, payment terms, or lease agreements
  • Neighborhood information (schools, transport, amenities)
  • Scheduling or rescheduling property visits
  • Any concerns or special requirements you may have

Chat Tips

  • Be clear and specific in your questions
  • Agents may not respond immediately - they'll get back to you as soon as possible
  • Your chat history is saved so you can refer back to previous conversations
  • Always be respectful and professional in your communications

Managing Your Account

Creating an Account

  • Click "Account" in the top navigation and select "Login / Signup"
  • Click "Sign Up" and fill in your details (name, email, password)
  • Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox
  • Log in with your credentials to access all features

Account Benefits

  • Book property visits and receive confirmations
  • Chat with agents and maintain conversation history
  • Write reviews for properties you've visited
  • Receive notifications about your bookings and messages

Logging In and Out

  • Click "Account" and select "Login" if you're not already signed in
  • Enter your email and password to access your account
  • To log out, hover over "Account" and click "Logout"

Forgot Your Password?

  • On the login page, click "Forgot Password?"
  • Enter your email address and submit
  • Check your email for a password reset link
  • Follow the instructions to create a new password

Writing and Reading Reviews

Reading Reviews

  • Scroll to the "House Reviews" section on any property details page
  • View the overall rating and read individual reviews from other users
  • Reviews from verified visits are marked with a checkmark
  • Click "See All Reviews" to view all reviews if there are more than 4

Writing a Review

  • You can write a review after booking and completing a visit
  • Click "Write a Review" on the property details page
  • Rate the property from 1 to 5 stars
  • Write your honest feedback about the property, agent service, and experience
  • Submit your review - it will be visible to other users immediately

Review Guidelines

  • Be honest and constructive in your feedback
  • Focus on your actual experience with the property
  • Avoid offensive language or personal attacks
  • Don't include personal contact information in reviews

Frequently Asked Questions

Is HomeLoop free to use?
Yes! HomeLoop is completely free for home seekers. You can search properties, save favorites, book visits, chat with agents, and write reviews at no cost.
Do I need an account to search properties?
No, you can browse and search properties without an account. However, you'll need to create a free account to book visits, chat with agents, and write reviews.
How do I know if a property is still available?
Each property listing shows the number of units available. You can also chat with the agent directly to confirm current availability and any upcoming vacancies.
What if the agent doesn't respond to my chat?
Agents typically respond within a few hours during business hours. If you don't receive a response within 24 hours, you can contact our support team for assistance.
Can I book multiple property visits?
Yes! You can book as many property visits as you like. We recommend booking several viewings to compare options and find the perfect home for you.
How do I report a problem with a listing?
If you notice incorrect information, suspicious activity, or inappropriate content, please contact our support team immediately at support@homeloop.com. We take all reports seriously and investigate promptly.
Are the agents on HomeLoop verified?
Yes, all agents on HomeLoop go through a verification process. We verify their credentials, business registration, and contact information to ensure you're working with legitimate professionals.
What payment methods are accepted?
Payment terms and methods are arranged directly between you and the agent/property owner. HomeLoop does not process payments. Always discuss payment details during your property visit or through agent communication.

Safety & Security Tips

Your safety is our priority. Follow these guidelines for a secure experience:

  • Always meet agents at the property during daylight hours when possible
  • Bring a friend or family member to property viewings
  • Verify agent credentials by checking their profile and reviews
  • Never make payments before viewing a property in person
  • Be cautious of deals that seem too good to be true
  • Keep all communication on the HomeLoop platform for your protection
  • Report any suspicious activity or listings to our support team
  • Read the lease agreement carefully before signing anything
  • Never share your bank PIN, passwords, or sensitive financial information

🛡️ Report Issues: If you encounter any suspicious listings, fraudulent activity, or feel unsafe during any interaction, please contact us immediately at support@homeloop.com. All reports are confidential.
