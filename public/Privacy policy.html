Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use HomeLoop.

1. Introduction

Welcome to HomeLoop. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform. By accessing or using HomeLoop, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled responsibly and in accordance with applicable data protection laws in Kenya.

2. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services:

Personal Information

  • Name, email address, and phone number when you create an account
  • Booking details and preferences when you schedule property visits
  • Communication history with agents through our chat feature
  • Profile information and user preferences

Usage Information

  • Device type, browser information, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent on the platform, and navigation patterns
  • IP address and general location information
  • Search queries and property viewing history

Agent Information

  • Business registration details and credentials
  • Property listings, photos, and descriptions
  • Professional contact information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account on HomeLoop
  • To connect you with property agents and facilitate bookings
  • To enable real-time chat communication between users and agents
  • To send booking confirmations, reminders, and important updates
  • To improve our platform functionality and user experience
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activities or security breaches
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize our services
  • To send promotional offers and newsletters (with your consent)

4. Sharing of Information

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal data to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

With Property Agents

  • Your contact information and booking details are shared with agents when you schedule property visits
  • Chat messages between you and agents are stored to facilitate communication

With Service Providers

  • Trusted third-party payment processors for secure transactions (if applicable)
  • Email and SMS service providers for sending notifications
  • Cloud hosting services for data storage and platform operation

Legal Requirements

  • Government authorities or law enforcement when required by Kenyan law
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of HomeLoop, our users, or the public
  • In connection with legal proceedings or investigations

5. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement various measures to protect it:

  • Encryption of sensitive data during transmission using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure servers with regular security updates and monitoring
  • Access controls to limit who can view your personal information
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Secure password storage using industry-standard hashing

Important: While we implement robust security measures, no online platform can guarantee 100% security. Please use strong passwords and never share your login credentials with anyone.

6. Cookies and Tracking

HomeLoop uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

Essential Cookies

  • Required for authentication and security
  • Enable core platform functionality
  • Remember your login session

Analytics Cookies

  • Help us understand how users interact with our platform
  • Allow us to improve user experience and performance
  • Provide insights into popular features and content

You can control cookies through your browser settings, though disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality.

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access and Portability

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format

Correction and Update

  • Update your profile information at any time
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data

Deletion

  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • We will retain some information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes

Opt-Out

  • Unsubscribe from promotional emails and newsletters
  • Disable non-essential cookies through browser settings
  • Restrict how we process your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

8. Children's Privacy

HomeLoop is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate action.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services and maintain your account
  • Comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes
  • Prevent fraud and enhance platform security
  • Improve our services through analysis

When you delete your account, we will remove or anonymize your personal data within 90 days, except where retention is required by law.

10. Third-Party Links

HomeLoop may contain links to third-party websites or services (such as payment processors or social media). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification to registered users
  • Displaying a prominent notice on our platform

Your continued use of HomeLoop after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

12. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Compliance)

For users in regions with data protection regulations, we process your personal data based on:

  • Consent: You have given clear consent for specific processing activities
  • Contract: Processing is necessary to fulfill our services to you
  • Legal Obligation: We must comply with applicable laws
  • Legitimate Interest: Processing is necessary for our legitimate business interests
