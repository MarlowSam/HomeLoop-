Our Mission

To revolutionize the property rental and sales market in Kenya by providing a transparent, efficient, and user-friendly platform that connects property seekers with verified agents and quality listings.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and comprehensive real estate platform in Kenya, empowering individuals to find their perfect home while enabling agents to grow their business through technology.

Our Story

HomeLoop was born from a simple observation: finding a home in Kenya shouldn't be a frustrating, time-consuming process filled with uncertainty and mistrust. Founded in 2024, we set out to create a platform that brings transparency, efficiency, and trust to the property market.

We understand the challenges both property seekers and agents face. For seekers, it's the endless scrolling through unreliable listings, dealing with unresponsive agents, and worrying about scams. For agents, it's reaching the right audience and managing inquiries efficiently.

HomeLoop bridges this gap by verifying every agent, ensuring property listings are accurate and up-to-date, and providing tools that make the entire process seamless—from browsing properties to booking site visits and communicating with agents in real-time.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of users across Kenya, helping families find their dream homes and agents grow their businesses. But we're just getting started.
